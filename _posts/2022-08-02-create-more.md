In this post I explain why this blog exists.

I want to do more creative activities, such as:

- Writing;
- Speaking;
- Drawing;
- Programming;
- Making music.

I want to do fewer passive activities, such as:

- Web browsing;
- Reading Hacker News;
- Watching Netflix;
- Gaming.

I want this because:

1. I feel good when being creative.
2. I want to connect with interesting people through my work.
3. I want to do good through creation.
4. I want control in my life. And doing it yourself is the price of freedom.

I created this blog because it is a motivation and a purpose for me to write.
